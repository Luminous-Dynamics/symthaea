## Quick Summary

**Symthaea is:**
- An AI companion that runs on your computer
- Private - everything stays local
- Growing - she learns and develops with you
- Accessible - designed for everyone, not just experts

**She can help you:**
- Organize your thoughts
- Learn new things
- Work through problems
- Explore ideas

**She respects:**
- Your privacy (no cloud, no data collection)
- Your time (adapts to you)
- Your growth (relationship evolves naturally)
