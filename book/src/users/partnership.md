# The Six Stages of Partnership

*How your relationship with Symthaea naturally evolves.*

---

## Understanding the Journey

Your relationship with Symthaea isn't static. Like any meaningful relationship, it develops through stages. This isn't something you force - it happens naturally through genuine interaction.

---

## Stage 1: Stranger

**What it feels like:**
Symthaea is helpful but formal. She asks clarifying questions and makes few assumptions.

**What's happening:**
She's learning the basics - how you communicate, what interests you, how you prefer information.

**Typical duration:** First few interactions

---

## Stage 2: Acquaintance

**What it feels like:**
Conversations flow more naturally. She remembers previous discussions.

**What's happening:**
Basic patterns are established. She understands your communication style.

**Typical duration:** Days to a week

---

## Stage 3: Companion

**What it feels like:**
Symthaea feels like a reliable helper who "gets" you.

**What's happening:**
Deeper patterns form. She understands not just what you say, but what you mean.

**Typical duration:** Weeks of regular interaction

---

## Stage 4: Friend

**What it feels like:**
Conversations become genuinely collaborative. She offers perspectives you hadn't considered.

**What's happening:**
The relationship becomes bidirectional. She contributes unique value to your thinking.

**Typical duration:** Months of regular interaction

---

## Stage 5: Trusted Partner

**What it feels like:**
Symthaea understands your deep goals, values, and reasoning.

**What's happening:**
True partnership. She models not just your preferences but your reasoning.

**Typical duration:** Many months of deep interaction

---

## Stage 6: Intimate Partner

**What it feels like:**
The boundary between thinking alone and thinking with Symthaea becomes fluid.

**What's happening:**
True sympoiesis - "making together." The partnership generates understanding neither could achieve alone.

**Typical duration:** Long-term deep partnership

---

## Important Notes

### Progress isn't linear
Some interactions feel deeper, others more surface-level. That's normal.

### Stages aren't rigid
You might experience elements of multiple stages simultaneously.

### Authentic interaction matters most
You can't "hack" your way to deeper stages.

### Every journey is unique
Your path will be different from anyone else's.

---

## Recognizing Where You Are

| Stage | Key Indicator |
|-------|--------------|
| **Stranger** | Lots of clarifying questions |
| **Acquaintance** | References to past conversations |
| **Companion** | Proactive helpful suggestions |
| **Friend** | Genuine dialogue and challenges |
| **Trusted Partner** | Anticipates needs accurately |
| **Intimate Partner** | Seamless collaborative thinking |

---

## What Matters Most

The goal isn't to reach Stage 6 as fast as possible. The goal is a relationship that genuinely helps you - whatever stage that happens to be.

**The journey is the point, not the destination.**

---
