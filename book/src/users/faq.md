# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## General

### What is Symthaea?
An AI companion that runs locally on your computer, designed to grow with you over time.

### Is Symthaea free?
Yes. Open source and freely available.

### Do I need internet?
Only for initial download. After that, completely offline.

### How is she different from ChatGPT?
- Runs locally (not cloud)
- Remembers you across sessions
- Designed for ongoing relationship
- No training on your data

---

## Privacy

### Does Symthaea collect data?
No. She can't send data anywhere.

### Can anyone see my conversations?
Only if they access your computer.

### What if I uninstall?
Delete the folder - no hidden data remains.

---

## Getting Started

### I'm not technical. Can I use Symthaea?
Setup currently requires some comfort with command line. We're working on easier options.

### How long to install?
About 10-15 minutes for someone comfortable with terminals.

### Do I need to train her?
No. She understands immediately.

---

## Using Symthaea

### What's the best way to start?
Just be natural. Ask questions, share thoughts, describe problems.

### Does she remember previous conversations?
Yes! Unlike most AI, Symthaea maintains continuous memory.

### Can I have multiple modes?
She adapts naturally to the conversation.

---

## Technical

### What is "consciousness" here?
Integrated information processing, measured mathematically. Not a claim about subjective experience.

### What does "Phi" mean?
A mathematical measure of how integrated a system's processing is.

### Why local instead of cloud?
Privacy, continuous relationship, and philosophical alignment with human autonomy.

---

## Relationship

### What do you mean by "partnership"?
Ongoing relationship that evolves, not transactional Q&A.

### Is she replacing human relationships?
No. She complements them, helps you process, think clearly.

### Can she help with emotions?
She can listen and help you process, but isn't a replacement for professional support.

## Problems

### She seems confused
Try rephrasing or providing more context.

### Responses are slow
Close other heavy apps, restart Symthaea.

### How do I report bugs?
GitHub issues page.
